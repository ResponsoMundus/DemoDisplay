Building Escape

密室逃脱

Build Escape is my first project built with Unreal Engine 4 and C++. In this game, the goal for the player is to find out how to interact with the scene and get out of the house.

密室逃脱是我第一次使用虚幻4引擎与C++开发的学习项目。而在这个小游戏里，玩家的目的很简单，就是要与场景互动并最终找到离开房间的方法。

I implemented a grab component for player to grab objects in the scene which is essential for player to escape from the room. There will be a hidden trigger plate implemented based on Trigger Volume provided by Unreal and player need to find a way to keep it triggered so that he or she could escape successfully.

通过C++，我实现了一个简单的物品抓取部件，使玩家操纵的角色可以抓取场景中的一些特殊物品。同时，我基于虚幻4引擎中自带的Trigger Volume实现了隐蔽重力触控板的功能， 并以此操纵场景中的一些可互动物品，比如门。

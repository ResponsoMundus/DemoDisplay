Toon Tanks

卡通坦克大战

Toon Tanks is the second project I developed with Unreal Engine 4 and C++. Player controls a cartoon-style tank and has the goal to destroy every enemy turrets to secure the area.

In this project, player controls the tank movement with classic WASD keyboard inputs and the turret of the tank is controlled by player cursor location. And hostile turrets are controlled by built-in AI of each turret so that they will detect the player and fire at the player's tank when they are in range.

I also tried to work with the gamemode and UI widget blueprints provided by Unreal Engine 4. I extended the default gamemode to record how many enemy is still alive and connected it with an UI widget that display information, such as game start count down and win or lose notifications, for the player.
